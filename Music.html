Exploration of my Music relation

I love listening to Music. It has so many positive effects on me that I have to listen to it every day. From helping me get a positive attitude to entertaining me, it provides it all. The Health Benefits of listening to music of North Shore University HealthSystem on the music page has more information of how listening to music is good for your health.

Major benefit of Music

Listening to music helps me in various ways but one of the best ways is to reduce my stress levels. At some times, I often get stressed maybe due to school works or just basic stuff, but listening to music helps me calm down and think away from the stress. It is a very calming and relaxing thing to do which reduces stress from your stress hormones in your body. It is definetly one of great ways of returning to a cooler mindset and having a great time while doing so.

The Genre type

The type of Music I mostly listen to is Pop or Hip Hop. I like the pop genre more because there are a lot of variety in the genre to listen to. In my opinion, pop is the best genre in music. It makes you happy and energetic. Many songs are fast-tempo and talk about believing in yourself. When I am having a bad day, I listen to my favourite songs from the pop genre and it uplifts my mood.
